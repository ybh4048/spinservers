## Who Is SpinServers?

SpinServers (operating as Majestic Hosting Solutions LLC) is a US-based Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) provider. What makes them a bit unusual: they're also one of the largest resellers of used enterprise server hardware globally through their sister company TheServerStore.com, which has been in the hardware business since 1994. That vertical integration is how they keep prices low while running real enterprise iron — Dell, HP, Quanta, Supermicro — instead of mystery-brand commodity boxes.

They operate two Tier III datacenters: **Dallas, TX** and **San Jose, CA** (Silicon Valley). They run their own ASN (AS 396073), with carriers including Cogent, HE, Level 3, and IX. The facilities are SOC2 certified, and HIPAA and PCI compliant.

## The Part Most Providers Charge Extra For (SpinServers Doesn't)

Three things that competitors treat as premium add-ons are standard here:

**Dual 10Gbps ports** — every dedicated server, even the $79 entry-level plan, ships with two 10Gbps network ports. No metered throttle, no "upgrade to 10G" upsell.

**Free Virtual Private Cloud (VPC)** — you can build L2 private networks between your servers within their infrastructure at no extra cost. Users on WebHostingTalk have called this feature "a game changer" — it lets you turn a handful of standalone servers into something resembling a private cloud without paying managed cloud prices.

**IPMI/iKVM access** — full out-of-band console access to your server is included. If you brick the OS, you don't need to open a ticket and wait for someone to plug in a keyboard. You log into the client portal and fix it yourself.
